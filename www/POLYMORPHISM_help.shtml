Interpreting Polymorphism Results

The top of the page indicates the official symbol, linked to its Detail page, and its name, MGI accession ID, and chromosomal location. Just below this section a table summarizes the polymorphism data.

The polymorphism assay table has the following fields and values:

ReferenceJ number of the supporting reference, linked to reference record.
ProbeProbe used to detect the polymorphism, linked to MGI probe or prime detail record.
EndonucleaseRestriction enzyme used to cut the DNA.
AlleleDesignation for the locus allele in which the polymorphism was found.
FragmentsDescription of the restriction fragments and PCR products
StrainsStrain names for the strains in which the given polymorphism was detected.
