How do I use the graph?

The graph shows the selected marker at the center, surrounded by the makers with which it is known to interact. The first 500 markers are shown based on how the table is sorted. The default sort is by score.

Click the plus sign to zoom in (2X) on the graph and the minus sign to zoom out (50%).

Click and hold to drag and reposition the graph.

How do I filter my results?

You can filter results by Type of Interaction, Level of Validation or Data Source. In the banner above the graph, after Filter interactions by: click the desired box. ***This will reduce your results to only those containing the checked items.

Top

How do I sort my results?

Click on any of the column headings or the up/down arrowheads ( ▼ ▲) to change the sort order of your results.
