New Gene Detail Page Features


MGI Gene Detail pages have always provided links to all the information we have on the mouse gene. With this release, the Detail pages display more information and provide more ways to view subsets of data and to access details.   2. A new section, Human Diseases, provides a table of human diseases the Online Mendelian Inheritance in Man (OMIM) database has associated with the human gene as well human diseases modeled in mice with mutations in the gene.
